Our Story

Silk & Strands was born from a desire to make wig-wearing effortless, comfortable, and beautiful.

We believe every woman deserves to feel confident without sacrificing the health of her natural hair. That's why all our wigs are made with breathable, vegan-friendly materials and the softest silk bases.

From our family to yours – we're here to help you switch up your style, protect your edges, and feel like the best version of you.
